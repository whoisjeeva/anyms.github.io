The future of artificial intelligence

with Paula García Esteban

Whether you are a person curious about new technologies and their impact on society, or if in your professional life you need to know the potential of artificial intelligence and incorporate it into your company's digitalization strategy, this course is for you. You will learn, through examples and curiosities, the main concepts of AI without mathematics or programming. During this course you will learn what it is and what it is not, some curiosities and main milestones in history, the most notable advances of the last decade and examples of how artificial intelligence is part of our daily lives.
